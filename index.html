HOPEC Insurance - Claim Information

To file an insurance claim, please review the information below and follow the instructions. Our team is dedicated to processing your claim quickly and efficiently.

Claim Process:

  1. Gather Documentation: Collect all relevant documents, such as police reports, medical bills, repair estimates, and any other supporting evidence.
  2. Complete the Claim Form: Fill out the claim form available on our website or at our office. Ensure all information is accurate and complete.
  3. Submit Your Claim: Submit the completed claim form and supporting documents to our claims department via email or mail.
  4. Review and Processing: Our claims adjusters will review your claim and contact you if additional information is needed.
  5. Claim Settlement: Once your claim is approved, we will process the payment according to your policy terms.

HOPEC Insurance - Pricing & Service Details

Below are the pricing details for our various insurance services. Please note that prices may vary based on individual circumstances and coverage options.

Life Insurance

  • Basic Plan: $1200/month - Covers up to $100,000 in the event of death.
  • Standard Plan: $2320/month - Covers up to $250,000, including accidental death and dismemberment.
  • Premium Plan: $6480/month - Covers up to $500,000, including critical illness coverage and family income benefit.

Health Insurance

  • Bronze Plan: $2240/month - Covers basic doctor visits, generic prescriptions, and preventive care.
  • Silver Plan: $4400/month - Covers specialist visits, brand-name prescriptions, and some diagnostic services.
  • Gold Plan: $6100/month - Covers comprehensive medical services, including hospitalization, surgery, and advanced diagnostics.

Auto Insurance

  • Basic Liability: $1160/month - Covers bodily injury and property damage liability.
  • Standard Coverage: $1780/month - Includes liability coverage, collision coverage, and comprehensive coverage (theft, vandalism).
  • Premium Coverage: $2940/month - Adds uninsured/underinsured motorist coverage, roadside assistance, and rental car reimbursement.

Custom Insurance

  • Custom Plan allows you to pay smaller amount from $200 to $2000/month, and we would find a very suitable insurance cover for that.
  • This insurance covers up from $25,000 to $80,000 in event of death or hospitalization or even auto insurance.

For more detailed information or to customize a plan to fit your needs, please contact our customer service team.
